General

I just graduated from Harvard ('21), where I received a B.A. in statistics and mathematics. I study methods in high-dimensional inference with Professor Lucas Janson. In fall 2021, I will start a PhD in statistics at Stanford.

Outside of research, I'm a classical pianist and flutist (there are an embarassing number of videos of my performances on youtube). In college, I also competed internationally for Harvard's debate team, and I try to remain (somewhat) active in that community. My other interests include public policy, economics, and fantasy/science fiction books.
